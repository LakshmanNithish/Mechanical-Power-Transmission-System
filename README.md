# Power Transmission Chassis Assembly

A CAD-based mechanical engineering project that demonstrates the design and assembly of a compact chassis integrated with a **90° mechanical power transmission system** using **SOLIDWORKS**. The project focuses on transmitting rotary motion between perpendicular shafts while integrating the mechanism into a structural chassis suitable for educational and prototype applications.

---

## Project Overview

The objective of this project is to design and assemble a mechanical system capable of transmitting rotational power through a **90° change in shaft direction**. The transmission mechanism is incorporated into a lightweight chassis to illustrate how powertrain components can be integrated within a structural framework.

The project emphasizes CAD modeling, mechanical assembly, motion transmission, and component integration while following standard engineering design practices.

---

## 90° Power Transmission Mechanism

The power transmission unit is the core of the assembly. It transfers rotational motion from an input shaft to an output shaft positioned at a right angle using interconnected rotating elements.

Unlike direct shaft coupling, this mechanism enables efficient transmission where the driving and driven shafts are perpendicular to each other, making it suitable for compact mechanical systems with space constraints.

## Chassis Assembly

The transmission mechanism is mounted on a fabricated chassis that provides structural support and maintains accurate alignment of all rotating components.

The chassis serves as the foundation of the system by securely positioning the shafts, bearings, transmission units, and wheels while ensuring proper load distribution during operation.

## Working Principle

The input shaft receives rotational motion, which is transferred through the 90° transmission mechanism to the driven shaft. Multiple transmission units are integrated into the chassis to distribute rotational power while maintaining proper shaft alignment.

The supporting frame ensures rigidity and minimizes unwanted movement, resulting in smooth and reliable power transmission throughout the assembly.
